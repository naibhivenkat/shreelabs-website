Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: July 2026


1. Overview

ShreeLabs provides both digital software services and marketplace solutions. Delivery of products may include physical goods supplied by registered local shop owners and digital services provided directly by ShreeLabs.


2. Physical Product Delivery

Orders placed through the Local Shop Finder platform are fulfilled and delivered by the respective registered shop owner or their authorized delivery personnel.

  • Delivery availability depends on the participating shop.
  • Estimated delivery times are displayed during checkout where applicable.
  • Delivery timelines may vary due to weather, traffic, holidays, or other operational factors.

3. Delivery Areas

Delivery services are available only in locations where registered partner shops operate. Availability may differ from one locality to another.


4. Digital Services

Digital products, subscriptions, and software services offered by ShreeLabs are delivered electronically. Access is typically provided immediately or within one business day after successful payment verification.


5. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges, if applicable, will be clearly displayed before order confirmation. Charges may vary depending on the shop, delivery distance, order value, and promotional offers.


6. Failed Deliveries

Delivery may fail if the provided address is incorrect, the customer is unavailable, or delivery cannot be completed for operational reasons. In such cases, the customer may be contacted for further assistance.


7. Order Tracking

Where supported by the platform, customers can view the current status of their orders from their account dashboard.
